Our Mission

Our Mission is to offer exceptional service, results and respect. I try to provide a peaceful atmosphere as I care for you hair. Each product is chosen and made with integrity and only the highest quality ingredients are used. All utensils are thoroughly sterilized or purchased new whenever possible for each client to ensure health and safety. It is my goal to ensure the care of my clients comes first.

Meet Your Stylist

My name is Ranae Gaffin. I was born in California and Raised in Virginia. I started doing hair as a way to support myself as a teen after my mother and father sent me to live with my aunt. Growing up I didn't have things easy and was always taught to work hard. I started practicing on my own hair and soon started doing my cousins hair. i started doing hair for the locals in the town. I later came back to california and started my company Loyalty To Locs.
